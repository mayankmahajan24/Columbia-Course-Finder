About

This web application was created by Mayank Mahajan.

To use this application, first select the constraints you would like to apply. You may also enter text in the input box that corresponds to the name of the course or the instructor. Then click submit to view the results. If there is a class you would like to add to your schedule, select the "Add to schedule" button on the left-most column of the screen. The course should appear on the top right under the "My Courses" section. You may deselect a course either through the "My Courses" section or through the table of results itself. To view your complete schedule, scroll down to the frame that contains the courses.adicu.com webpage.

If you have any questions or feedback, please click the Feedback/Questions tab and comment through Facebook in the window. If you wish to remain anonymous, please email your feedback to mm4399@columbia.edu.

Thank you for using the Columbia Course Finder!
